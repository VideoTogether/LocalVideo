VideoTogether Plugin Copyright Notice

The video download functionality of the VideoTogether plugin is developed based on the standard APIs provided by browsers. While we have preemptively blocked several well-known copyrighted websites, we cannot guarantee complete coverage of all copyrighted content.

If you are a content provider or website owner and do not wish users to utilize this plugin's download function on your site, please take the following measures:

  1. Integrate the following code into the javascript of your website to temporarily disable the plugin's download capability: 
    window.VideoTogetherDownload = 'disabled';
  2. In addition, please visit https://github.com/VideoTogether/VideoTogether/issues to create an issue and provide your website domain. We pledge to add your website to our plugin's built-in blocklist within two business days upon receiving the notification.

We respect the copyright of every content provider and are committed to collaborating with all parties to ensure the lawful sharing and use of online content.
